OUR PROGRAM

3-Month Courses & Internship Program

Our training and internship program is designed to transform beginners into industry-ready professionals. The first month focuses on intensive hands-on training with expert mentor guidance on your chosen track. The remaining 2 months offer internship promotion based on your skills, performance, and consistency.

You'll gain real-world experience working on live projects, receive industry-recognized certificates, a lifetime Udemy course, 24/7 mentor support, resume building assistance, and direct job opportunities — all in one program.

  • Experience Letter, Completion Certificate & Internship Certificate
  • 1 Lifetime Udemy Course, 24/7 Mentor Support & Resume Building
  • Letter of Recommendation, Job Opportunities & Internal Hackathons
